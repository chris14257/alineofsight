Welcome

Chris Mairs

Welcome to the website for my 3,600 mile tandem ride across America in summer 2017, raising funds to restore sight to 3,600 cataract sufferers around the world. 

Being blind myself, I believe passionately in transforming lives by restoring sight for as many people as we possibly can. My own sight is unlikely to be fixable in my own lifetime as I have a rare hereditary disorder. However, cataracts can be fixed by simple surgical intervention, and I intend to fund 3,600 such interventions, one for each mile that I ride.

We will do this through co-operation with See International on many projects throughout 2017. For more details on See International and the projects we are funding see our Use of Funds page
